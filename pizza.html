Cheese Pizza

Description

Made with homemade pizza dough then topped with two different cheeses, this recipe will not disappoint!
Get ready to indulge in this cheesy meal for pizza night!

Ingredients

  • 1 standard pack of yeast
  • 1 and 1/3 cups of water (Warm - 100-110F)
  • Unbleached all-purpose white flour
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • Salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • Cornmeal
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the dough ingredients together by hand or use a hand-held or stand mixer.
  2. Knead by hand or with your mixer.
  3. Place dough into a greased mixing bowl, cover tightly, and set aside to rise for about 90 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge.
  5. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
  6. Sprinkle with parmesan before baking for 12 to 14 minutes (sprinkle cornmeal on baking pan/sheet), or until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbling.
